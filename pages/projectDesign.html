Belo Branding Story

“Belo” is not just a name; it also tells a historical and personal story! Belo has been my nickname since childhood; it was my brothers who chose it; it describes friendship, soul mates, and brotherhood, regarding “by” which means that the project is owned by me, Belo. My cultural inheritance was strongly affected by the environment around me. I started my youth journeys by riding horses at Lagos Polo Club, which was an unforgettable experience.

Brand Overview

All types of "Bybelo" Saffron are carefully picked from each harvest of the unique Moroccan farms. We provide our excellent Saffron only during its specific season, quickly plucked and perfectly prepared to ensure pure organic quality. Our main partners are the Berber women, and we gladly buy their own unique products provided by their farms, as we annually offer them 10% of the profits. We appreciate Saffron's health benefits, besides its tremendous impact on creating different dishes, cosmetics, art, and various precious herbs.
